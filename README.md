# Bubble Pop! 🫧

A cute, pastel bubble-popping game. Pop shapes, build combos, chase your high score.

**Play now →** https://sophian-dev.github.io/bubble-pop

## Features
- Hearts, stars, flowers, moons & diamonds to pop
- Combo multiplier (up to 5x!)
- High score board with celebration animations
- Squeaky toy sound effects
- Dark mode toggle
- Installable as a phone app (PWA)
- Share your score with friends
